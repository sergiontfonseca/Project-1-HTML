Products

Digital Printing

Digital printing products are printed in digital machines via laser or inkjet technologies and are much cheaper to setup but more expensive per print.

This means that for less quantities (typically below 1000 copies) digital printing is cheaper

It's also faster to print due to alien tech

The quality is extremely good but offset printing offers perfect printing quality

Offset Printing

Offset printing products are printed in machines via mechanical technologies that use ink which is more expensive to setup but cheaper per print.

This means that for more quantities (typically over 1000 copies) offset printing is cheaper

It's slower to print due to lack of alien tech

The quality of printing is perfect since you can manipulate the ink to be exactly what you expect
